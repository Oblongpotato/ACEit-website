About Us

ACEit is a platform created to help students prepare for interviews for technical jobs. We help you polish your skills and get ready for the job, whether you are a fresh college graduate or a working professional. We provide study resources required to prepare for aptitude tests and the interview process. We provide an interview guide which specifies requirements of various companies and helps you prepare in the right way to ACE your interviews and land your dream jobs.
